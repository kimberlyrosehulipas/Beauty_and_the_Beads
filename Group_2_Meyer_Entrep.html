OUR BUSINESS PLAN

COMPANY:  Beauty and the Beads


Beads have been a part of our fashion industry for a long period of time. It has been a symbolism of accessory trends usually built as small perforated spheres. In accordance with this, Beauty and the Beads is one uprising independent enterprise that aims to offer high-quality affordable accessories personally handcrafted by the company. It includes various bead shapes, sizes, and colors assembled altogether by a thread, wire or string. The enterprise categorizes itself under a type of business model called the “Manufacturer Model”. This model intends to create a product from raw materials. Furthermore, it can also be considered as a business to consumer model, this focuses to provide service or product directly to satisfy the needs and wants of the consumer.

Subsequently, for the past few years ever since the pandemic had emerged to spread around the whole world, a huge number of people are prohibited to go out of their respective houses. Now, given that the vaccine is continuously distributed, a great adjustment of freedom is given to the society. This simply means that a lot are eager to present themselves outside of their houses with their newly improved fashion sense. This opportunity is great for small businesses related to jewelry and accessories like Beauty and the Beads so that consumers can flourish their basic outfits to a great representation of bewildering fashion sense. Additionally, Beauty and the Beads is also open to offer customizable accessories that can be personally customized by consumers themselves. They guarantee that the product is less expensive and more family-friendly that can be suitable for people ages 7 and above. The company is also open to criticisms and suggestions that consumers have.

Beauty and the Beads would manage their sales and marketing through the use of social networks and internet marketing. They would use numerous social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram for their advertisements. In accordance with that, preparation and release of social media accounts about the enterprise is also awaited. The content includes the background of the company, the members included, and of course the product itself. The form for orders will also be indicated in the company’s social media accounts.

For the second week of April 2022, the company finished their market research to determine consumer behavior. It was successfully completed through a survey and analyzed by the company members. Moving forward, during this similar week of April, the prototype of the product was accomplished. Further release of the product and marketing will be discussed in the near time.

Production Process and Controls

Below is the procedure for making the beaded accessories for Beauty and the Beads:

  1. Prepare the materials.
    1. Gather the different types and colors of beads, elastic cords or wires, scissors, pliers, and other necessities.
  2. Plan the design.
    1. The design may be personalized as instructed by the customer or chosen from the sample products.
    2. The arrangement of beads will be evaluated and laid together to create a pattern and visualize the finished product before putting in the cord or wire.
  3. Cut the cord or wire to length.
    1. Scissors are to be used for elastic cords while pliers are for wires that cannot be cut by scissors otherwise.
  4. String the beads onto the cord or wire.
    1. Hold one end of the cord in one hand and use the other to place the beads.
    2. If the holes of the beads are not large enough, a needle may be utilized.
    3. Keep adding the beads until it is enough and occasionally check if it is too tight or too loose.
    4. Wires may also be shaped using pliers for appropriate accessories such as earrings.
  5. Secure the beading cord or wire.
    1. For normal beaded accessories, knot the ends of the cord or wire together.
    2. Secure the knot with a small amount of glue.
    3. Hide the knot inside one of the beads or by using a crimp cover.
    4. For earrings or similar, secure the wire with a hook or pin.
  6. Double-check the finished beaded product.
    1. Check whether the design and arrangement of beads are correct and if there are no errors.
  7. Fix the product into the package accordingly.

The accessory-making quality control will be ensured in each step of the production process, from securing the good condition of each bead to the overall quality of the beaded products such as bracelets, earrings, and necklaces. The accessories will be handmade and meticulously crafted by each member of the group. As such, the beads will be inspected to ensure that the following have the proper color, size, and type for each accessory, without damage. The quality of the cord and wire is also important as it holds the beads together and thus, makes or breaks the accessory. Therefore, to guarantee that the beaded products will be in pristine condition, these will be assessed by the members of the group, compared with the current beaded accessories in the market, and seek feedback to further improve their quality. Apart from Shopee, personal canvassing of the beads, cords, and wires in Pasig Mega Market will be done.

Marketing Strategy

Social media is a virtual space that has grown and evolved to its full efficiency, where social networking is not only the sole basis of its usage but can also be used to promote businesses and their respective products. Furthermore, it reaches a wide audience in just seconds of posting ads without having to spend a single dime.

With that being said, Beauty and the Beads will be making use of social media as its marketing platform. This will help the marketers build and establish a relationship with their consumers. The marketers will be able to engage with their customers on a regular basis. As student entrepreneurs, we consider this an advantage and we will make use of this chance to sell our goods more cost-effectively using the various advertising tools accessible on these social media sites. Simply being consistent and publishing relevant and relatable content for our audience can certainly help grow our social media page and business.

The platforms that will be used for social media marketing are Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.


Sales Strategy

The sales strategies that the business revolves around are affordability, durability, and customer satisfaction. Normally, cost-effective jewelry tends to wear off after some time. Beauty and the Beads will make sure that their products are less likely to break, shatter, or bend than its rivals in the market. The jewelries sold are extremely long-lasting in spite of its low price point, this means that the jewelry will be able to withstand constant use. With this, customer satisfaction will be achieved.


Product Features

The following are the product features that distinguish Beauty and the Beads from its industry rivals:

  1. Affordability - the products will be set out to the market at a low cost. However, this will not affect the quality and the integrity of the product itself. This offers a sustainable fashion that consumers won’t have to break the bank for.
  2. Variation - Beauty and the Beads offers products that are unique from one another. Each piece of jewelry will have its own distinction, and no piece shall look alike. This ensures that the customers will be presented with an array of products that they can choose from that fit their respective preferences.
  3. Customizable - the ability of a business to customize its products establishes uniqueness as opposed to its rivals. Moreover, fashion is never included in a one-size-fits-all narrative. With this, Beauty and the Beads shall offer customization and personalization services to its customers in order to tailor the products according to each person’s liking.
