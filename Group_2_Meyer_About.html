The management team of Beauty and the Beads comprises eight (8) co-owning senior year students from 12-Meyer of Pasig City Science High School. The team is responsible for the creation, advertisement, and overall management of the products, employing a partnership legal structure in which management duties, expenses, liabilities, and profits are equally divided and shared between the students. The business will be home-based and advertised online using social media platforms like Facebook as consulted by their Entrepreneurship teacher, Mrs. Beverly Rose Ramos. The purchased product will be delivered accordingly after transacting with the customer. Suppliers, delivery and logistics personnel, and other key figures are also involved in the smooth-sailing operation of Beauty and the Beads.

In correspondence to this, the members of the team are assigned in distinct positions in handling responsibility for each sector of task in the company.

Beauty and the beads began to arise from small ideas to one big picture, building confidence for the people and the company through the shared process of adding creativity and uniqueness of ideas from everyone included. In the earliest days of this journey, several students from Grade 12 Meyer gathered altogether to find an idea. These following students are known to be creative, hardworking, and witty people that collectively contributed to one another their ideas about a potential business to run.
